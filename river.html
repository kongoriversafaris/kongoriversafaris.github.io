Kongoriver Excursion

$20 / Per person

Trip Overview

Embark on an unforgettable experience with our Kongo River Excursion, crafted for nature enthusiasts and sunset seekers. Lasting approximately 2-3 hours, this adventure begins with a convenient pickup from your place of stay in Diani to the pristine Kongo Beach. Upon arrival, step onto a traditional canoe and sail along the river. Navigate through the lush mangroves, soaking in the serene ambiance while marveling at the vibrant birdlife that inhabits this scenic ecosystem.
Your voyage culminates in a breathtaking sunset spectacle, a perfect tableau against the river's backdrop, creating memories that linger long after. As dusk settles in, relish a tranquil interlude by a beach bonfire, a serene moment to reflect and unwind amidst the captivating rhythm of the waves. Finally, our journey concludes with a return ride to your place of stay, leaving you with cherished memories of a mesmerizing adventure along the Kongo River. View the full Itinerary Here!
