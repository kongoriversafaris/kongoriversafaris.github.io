Trip to Chale Island

Trip Code:E004

Trip Overview

Embark on an unforgettable full-day adventure as we take you on a thrilling snorkeling excursion to Chale Island. This tropical paradise, located in the heart of the Indian Ocean, promises a day of excitement, relaxation, and exploration. Get ready to set sail on a speedboat, discover vibrant marine life beneath the crystal-clear waters, savor a delicious lunch, and create lasting memories.

The Itinerary

Morning Departure: Your journey begins with a convenient pick-up from your place of stay in Diani. We'll whisk you away to our embarkation point, where our comfortable speedboat awaits.
Sailing to Chale Island: As we cruise over the azure waters of the Indian Ocean, take in the breathtaking coastal views. The speedboat ride to Chale Island is not only swift but also exhilarating. The sea breeze and warm sun create the perfect setting for your adventure.
Welcome to Chale Island: Upon arrival at Chale Island, you'll be greeted by the soft, powdery sand and the warm hospitality of The Sands at Chale Hotel. This exclusive beachfront property is your oasis for the day. You're invited to make full use of all the amenities, including sun loungers, beach activities, and the sparkling pool.
Lunch at The Sands at Chale Restaurant: Enjoy a delectable lunch at The Sands at Chale Restaurant, where you'll savor a variety of culinary delights. Satisfy your taste buds with fresh seafood, international dishes, and exotic flavors. The restaurant offers stunning views of the ocean, making your meal even more memorable.
Snorkeling Adventure: After lunch, it's time for the highlight of the day. Dive into the crystal-clear waters surrounding Chale Island and explore the vibrant underwater world. Our experienced tour guide will accompany you, ensuring your safety and helping you discover the stunning marine life and colorful coral reefs.
Afternoon Relaxation: Spend the rest of the afternoon at your own pace. Whether you choose to relax on the beach, swim in the ocean, or explore the lush island surroundings, you're free to enjoy this paradise as you please.
Sailing Back to Diani: As the day comes to an end, we'll board the speedboat for a scenic ride back to Diani. Relive your snorkeling adventure, cherish the memories you've made, and take in the mesmerizing coastal vistas.

The trip is inclusive of:

  • Pick-up and drop-off from your place of stay in Diani.
  • Speedboat ride to Chale Island.
  • Expert tour guide for snorkeling.
  • Access to all amenities at The Sands at Chale Hotel.
  • Delicious lunch at The Sands at Chale Restaurant.
important note:

  • The excursion is priced for a group of four individuals. If your group is larger, additional participants can join at a cost of USD 80 per person.
  • We recommend bringing sunscreen, a hat, sunglasses, swimwear, and a sense of adventure. Snorkeling equipment is provided.
  • Please book in advance to secure your spot on this incredible journey.
