5 days 4 nights in Diani Beach

$600 / Per 2 guests

Transportation: VIP SGR Ticket
Accommodation: Seaview Studio Appartment
Transfers: Private Transfer
Activities: 5 guided Excursions
Trip Description

Experience the allure of Diani Beach like never before with our exclusive 5-day, 4-night tailored package for two. Dive into crystal-clear waters, explore hidden caves, and unwind in a sea-view studio apartment as you indulge in the ultimate beach getaway. Immerse yourself in five captivating excursions, from snorkeling at Tiwi Beach's natural wonders to birdwatching along the Kongo River and a mesmerizing sunset sail through mangroves. Delight in a full-day adventure at Kisite Mpunguti Marine Park and Wasini Island for dolphin encounters and pristine snorkeling. Round it off with a Robinson Island glass-bottom boat trip for an enchanting sandbank experience. Join us for a meticulously crafted itinerary brimming with exploration, relaxation, and cherished memories in the breathtaking beauty of Diani Beach.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Diani Beach
- Departure from Nairobi via the SGR VIP Coach.
- Private transfers from the terminus to your studio apartment with a stunning sea view.
- Check-in and free time to relax after the Journey.

Day 2: Tiwi Beach Caves and Africa Pool Excursion
- visit to the Tiwi Beach Caves and the naturally curved Map of Africa pool for snorkeling and swimming.
- Explore the stunning underwater world and enjoy the natural formations.
- Enjoy a swahili food luch as part of the tour (food tour).
: Kongo River Birdwatching and Mangrove Excursion
- Embark on a serene boat ride along the Kongo River for birdwatching, mangroves exploration and witness the breathtaking sunset while sailing. Conclude the evening with a beach bonfire and dinner served from a nearby restaurant.

Day 3: Kisite Mpunguti Marine Park and Wasini Island
- Full-day excursion to Kisite Mpunguti Marine Park and Wasini Island.
- Experience dolphin watching, snorkeling in crystal-clear waters, and exploring the vibrant marine life.
- Enjoy a delicious lunch served as part of the package.

Day 4: Robinson Island Glass Bottom Boat and Sandbank Excursion
- Embark on a glass bottom boat ride to Robinson Island for snorkeling and relaxation on a beautiful sandbank.
- Immerse yourself in the underwater wonders and soak in the pristine surroundings.
- Enjoy another mement of local food tour before Returning to the apartment for the last evening in Diani Beach.

Day 5: Departure
- Check-out from the studio apartment.
- Private transfers to the SGR terminus for your return journey to Nairobi.

Package Inclusions:
  • Round-trip SGR VIP Coach tickets.
  • Private transfers to and from the terminus.
  • Accommodation in a studio apartment with sea-view for four nights.
  • Five exciting guided excursions exploring the natural beauty and marine life of Diani Beach.
  • Inclusive lunches for three days during excursions.
Notes:
  • Additional meals, apart from the specified lunches, are not included.
  • The itinerary can be adjusted based on weather conditions and availability.
  • Guests are encouraged to bring suitable attire for water activities and sunscreen for sun protection.
