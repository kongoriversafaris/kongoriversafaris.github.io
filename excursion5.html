Scuba Diving

Trip Code:E005

Trip Overview

Discover the mesmerizing underwater world of Diani Beach through our exhilarating Scuba Diving excursion. Dive into the crystal-clear depths of the Indian Ocean and experience the thrill of exploring Kenya's vibrant marine life. Our tour, in partnership with Kenya's premier diving center, offers a unique opportunity for both seasoned divers and beginners to immerse themselves in this captivating underwater adventure.

The Itinerary

Pickup: Your day begins with a convenient pickup from your place of stay in Diani.
Welcome to The Crab Diving Center: Upon arrival, you will be greeted by our friendly and experienced diving instructors at The Crab Diving Center. Here, you'll receive a brief orientation and safety briefing.
Gear Up: Get ready for the underwater adventure of a lifetime as you gear up with high-quality scuba equipment.
Guided Dive: Embark on a thrilling guided scuba dive in the pristine waters of the Indian Ocean. Marvel at the kaleidoscope of colors and diverse marine species that inhabit the coral reefs.
Exploration and Adventure: Explore the vibrant underwater world, witness coral formations, and interact with the enchanting marine life. Your dive will be led by certified instructors who will ensure your safety and enjoyment.
Return to The Crab Diving Center: After an unforgettable dive, return to The Crab Diving Center to relax and reflect on your underwater experiences.
Drop-off: We will provide transportation back to your place of stay, ensuring a convenient end to your scuba diving adventure.

Package is Inclusive of:

  • Professional Guidance: Our excursion includes expert guidance from certified diving instructors who will accompany you throughout the dive.
  • High-Quality Equipment: All necessary scuba diving equipment, including masks, fins, tanks, and wetsuits, is provided to ensure your safety and comfort.
  • Transportation: Enjoy a hassle-free experience with pickups and drop-offs from your accommodation to The Crab Diving Center.
  • Memories to Last a Lifetime: Capture the magic of the underwater world with an optional photography package to immortalize your diving experience.
  • Refreshments: Stay hydrated with complimentary water and light snacks provided during the excursion.

Note:

  • - Our Scuba Diving excursion is designed for those who want to explore the incredible underwater world of Diani without the need for formal training. However, if you are interested in pursuing a diving course, our partners at The Crab Diving Center can provide guidance and assistance to help you get started on your journey to becoming a certified diver.

Don't miss this opportunity to immerse yourself in the beauty of the Indian Ocean's underwater realm. Join us for a Scuba Diving adventure that will leave you with lasting memories of Diani's incredible marine life. Book your excursion today and get ready for an unforgettable dive!
