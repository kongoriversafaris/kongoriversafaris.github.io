Saltlick Lodge

Trip Overview

Discover the captivating allure of the Salt Lick Wilderness Lodge, a remarkable natural wonder nestled in the heart of Kenya's iconic Tsavo National Park. This unique safari destination offers an extraordinary wildlife experience that is sure to leave a lasting impression.


Wildlife Paradise: The Salt Lick Lodge is strategically built beside a natural salt lick, a mineral deposit that attracts a diverse array of wildlife, including elephants, giraffes, zebras, and more. From your comfortable lodge, witness this breathtaking spectacle as these magnificent creatures gather for a mineral-rich feast.
Treehouse Accommodation:Stay in one-of-a-kind treehouse accommodations elevated above the ground, providing unmatched views of the surrounding savannah and wildlife. Immerse yourself in the sounds and sights of the African wilderness from your private balcony.
Game Drives: Embark on exhilarating game drives in Tsavo National Park with experienced guides. Explore the park's stunning landscapes and encounter the "Big Five" and a multitude of other fascinating creatures.
Birdwatching: With over 300 bird species, Tsavo is a haven for birdwatchers. Capture the vibrant plumage and melodic songs of East African birdlife as you explore the diverse ecosystems.
Photography Opportunity: Whether you're an amateur or professional photographer, the photographic opportunities here are unparalleled. The unique surroundings and abundant wildlife make for exceptional photo captures.
Relaxation and Dining: After a day of adventure, unwind in the lodge's comfortable lounge, or enjoy delectable cuisine at the on-site restaurant. Savor international and local dishes while overlooking the untamed wilderness.

The Salt Lick Wilderness Lodge offers an unrivaled safari experience, combining breathtaking natural phenomena, luxury treehouse living, and unparalleled wildlife encounters. It's a destination that appeals to nature lovers, wildlife enthusiasts, and anyone seeking an unforgettable journey into the heart of Africa's untamed beauty.Prepare to be enchanted by the rhythmic pulse of the African wilderness, the stars above your treehouse, and the majesty of the animal kingdom. Don't miss the opportunity to make lasting memories at this remarkable salt lick destination in Tsavo National Park. Book your adventure today and let the wild magic of Africa unfold before your eyes.
