Wasini Island

Trip Code:E006

Trip Overview

Experience the magic of the ocean with our "Wasini Dolphin Trip," a full-day adventure that will take you on a journey through the azure waters of the Indian Ocean. Discover the wonders of marine life at Kisite Mpunguti Marine Park, encounter playful dolphins, indulge in a sumptuous seafood feast, and explore the enchanting Wasini Island. This exciting excursion is the perfect way to make lasting memories while basking in the beauty of the Kenyan coast.

The Itinerary

1. Pick-up from Your Accommodation: Our day begins with a comfortable pick-up from your place of stay. Our experienced and friendly guides will ensure a smooth start to your adventure.
2. Drive to Shimoni: Sit back and enjoy the scenic drive to Shimoni, where your maritime journey will commence. Along the way, you'll be treated to breathtaking views of the Kenyan coastline and lush landscapes.
3. Boat Ride to Kisite Mpunguti Marine Park: Upon reaching Shimoni, board a well-equipped boat that will take you to the world-renowned Kisite Mpunguti Marine Park. As you sail through the crystal-clear waters, keep an eye out for the charismatic dolphins that inhabit these waters. There's a remarkable 98% chance of spotting these playful creatures.
4. Snorkeling Adventure: Gear up for an underwater adventure as you snorkel in the pristine waters of the marine park. Explore the vibrant coral reefs and encounter an array of colorful marine life. If you're an adept swimmer, you may even have the opportunity to swim with the dolphins for a truly magical experience.
5. Sail to Wasini Island for Lunch: After your snorkeling escapade, sail to the idyllic Wasini Island, a tranquil paradise surrounded by the Indian Ocean. Here, you'll savor a delectable seafood lunch, featuring the freshest catches from the sea. Enjoy your meal in a serene and picturesque setting.
6. Return to Shimoni: With your appetite satisfied, it's time to head back to Shimoni. As you sail, take in the breathtaking views and relish the tranquility of the ocean.
7. Drive Back to Your Accommodation: Our guides will accompany you on the drive back to your place of stay, ensuring you reach your destination safely and with a heart full of unforgettable memories.

The trip is inclusive of:

  • Round-trip transportation from your accommodation.
  • Knowledgeable guides to enhance your experience.
  • Park fee at KWS checkpoint
  • Boat ride to Kisite Mpunguti Marine Park.
  • Snorkeling equipment for an underwater adventure.
  • A chance to swim with dolphins (for proficient swimmers).
  • Snacks to keep you warmed up before feat time
  • Delicious seafood lunch on Wasini Island.
  • Bottled water to keep you refreshed throughout the day.

Note:

  • - This trip is suitable for individuals and families looking for a memorable day of exploration and relaxation.
  • - Snorkeling is a thrilling experience for both beginners and experienced snorkelers.
  • - Dolphin encounters are subject to the animals' natural behavior and cannot be guaranteed.
  • - Kindly bring swimwear, sunscreen, a hat, and your sense of adventure!
  • - Our dedicated team is committed to ensuring your safety and enjoyment throughout the journey.
  • - Personal safety and Environmental care is crucial, "lets explore responsibly"

Book your "Wasini Dolphin Trip" with us today and embark on a day of aquatic wonder and culinary delight. Immerse yourself in the enchanting beauty of the Kenyan coast and create cherished memories that will last a lifetime.
