Map of Africa Pool

$25 / Per person

Trip Overview

Experience the natural wonders of Tiwi Beach like never before with our Map of Africa Pool excursion. This 3-hour adventure takes you on a journey of discovery, combining the thrill of snorkeling in mystical caves and the enchanting beauty of swimming in a pool naturally shaped like the African continent. Immerse yourself in the stunning marine life and geological formations that make this destination a hidden gem on the Kenyan coast.

The Itinerary

Pick Up: Your adventure begins with a convenient pick-up from your place of stay. We'll ensure you're comfortable and ready for the exploration ahead.
Snorkeling in Caves: Dive into the depths of Tiwi Beach's enchanting caves, where you'll discover a world teeming with colorful marine life. Our provided snorkels and reef shoes make this experience safe and accessible for all skill levels.
Swimming in Map of Africa Pool: After exploring the caves, enjoy a refreshing swim in the Map of Africa Pool, a natural wonder that offers a unique perspective of the African continent's shape. It's an unforgettable photo opportunity and a chance to relax in the pristine waters of this geological marvel.
Drop-off: We'll return you to your place of stay, ensuring you have cherished memories and captivating stories to share.

Note:


  • - Pricing: All prices are set in USD. Kenyan residents will be quoted in Kenyan Shillings (KSH) based on the current exchange rate.
  • - Tide Dependency: Please note that this excursion is tide-dependent. To ensure you have the best experience, we recommend booking at least a day in advance. This allows us to provide you with the optimal tide schedule for your journey.
  • - Children: Children aged 0-5 years are not charged for this excursion, while children aged 6-11 years are eligible for a special rate, priced at 75% of the adult fare.
