Robinson Island (Sandbank)

Trip Code:E002

Trip Overview

Discover the breathtaking underwater world of the Indian Ocean like never before with our Robinson Island tour. This 3-hour adventure combines the thrill of sailing on a glass-bottom boat with the charm of exploring a pristine sandbank hidden within the ocean during low tides. Experience the magic of snorkeling amidst colorful marine life, or simply relax on the sandbank, soaking in the beauty of nature. At the end of this unforgettable journey, you'll cruise back to the mainland, leaving you with memories that will last a lifetime.

The Itinerary

Departure & Glass-Bottom Boat Sailing: Your excursion begins with a convenient pick-up from your place of stay. You'll set off on a glass-bottom boat that offers a crystal-clear view of the underwater world. Marvel at the mesmerizing coral reefs, schools of fish, and other marine wonders as you make your way to Robinson Island.
Sandbank Exploration: Upon reaching Robinson Island, you'll disembark onto the pristine sandbank. During low tide, this secluded paradise emerges, providing the perfect setting for relaxation and exploration. You can sunbathe, swim, or play beach games while savoring the idyllic surroundings.
Snorkeling Adventure: For those seeking adventure beneath the waves, we offer a snorkeling experience that allows you to get up close and personal with the marine life of the Indian Ocean. Dive into the crystal-clear waters and discover a world of vibrant corals and exotic fish. Snorkeling equipment is provided, making it accessible for all experience levels.
Return to the Mainland: After a memorable time on the sandbank, you'll board the glass-bottom boat again for a scenic ride back to the mainland. This return journey allows you to enjoy more marine sights through the glass bottom, making your way back to Diani Beach.

The trip is inclusive of:

  • Pick-Up & Drop-Off: We offer convenient pick-up and drop-off services from your place of stay, ensuring a hassle-free experience.
  • Glass-Bottom Boat Ride: Sail in a specially designed glass-bottom boat that offers an immersive view of the underwater world.
  • Experienced Tour Guide: Our knowledgeable and friendly tour guide will accompany you throughout the excursion, providing insights and ensuring your safety.
Note:

  • Pricing: The prices for our Kongoriver Excursion: Robinson Island are set in USD. However, residents can inquire about the equivalent prices in KSH to make the booking process more convenient.
  • Tide Dependency: Please note that the excursion's timing is tide-dependent. To ensure the best experience, we recommend booking the day before to receive information about the ideal departure time.
  • Children's Pricing: Children aged 0-5 are not charged for this excursion. Children aged 6-11 can join the adventure at 75% of the adult price, making this excursion perfect for families looking for an unforgettable experience.

Don't miss the chance to explore the wonders of the Kongoriver Excursion: Robinson Island. Book your adventure today and create lasting memories in the heart of the Indian Ocean.
